Location: Andhra Pradesh - India

The first project where I had a managerial role, representing the Environmental Engineering team. Working on the 5 office towers, I worked both on external comfort and on building performance where I was able to impact the cladding design. It was the largest project where I had to coordinate with the MEP team and was leading all the communications with the design team and the client.
