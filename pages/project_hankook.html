Notable Projects

Category : High End Office smart Cladding system
Employer : Foster + Partners
Client: Hankook Tire
Location: Seoul - South Korea
Role : Design optimisation | Visual/thermal Specifications | Coordination with local Manufacturer | Site inspection

This is one of the most interesting projects I had the chance to work on and coordinate for the Environmental Engineering team at F+P. We had to come up with a fixed louvre system that would tackle glare and retain good daylight conditions throughout the space. I was able to push the boundaries of cladding specifications after doing thorough research and studies. I was able to alter the design to meet the need for glare mitigation as well as coordinate with the local manufacturer to build the system as specified.
Finally I was lucky enough to visit the site for visual inspection of the system. I led the communications with the client on the subject throughout this process.
