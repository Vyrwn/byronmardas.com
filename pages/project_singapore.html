Notable Projects

Category : Daylight | Glare | External Dazle
Employer : Foster + Partners
Client: Apple Inc.
Location: Singapore
Role : Environmental Studies | Coordination with design team

Probably the most high end project I have worked for. My role in this changed throughout the duration, starting from simple daylight studies evolving to general coordination of environmental design up to communication with city planning.
Due to the form of the building, glare mitigation measures were important and I was able, from early stages, to affect the design of the cladding system. Extensive studies around glare, daylight, device glare, screen viewing were done in multiple stages of the project. While in construction I had to coordinate with the team to ensure that no external dazle occurs to the surrounding areas.
