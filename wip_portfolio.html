I'm an Architect | Environmental Designer | Digital Computational Designer from London, UK. With experience in a big variety of small & large scale, single & mixed use buildings as well as masterplan level, I aim to make a difference through research informed design.

My goal is to enable better integration of environmental thinking as well as overall building performance, during the design process with the use of parametric and dynamic techniques. I am passionate with day to day problem solving, providing solutions for a series of environmental parameters, including daylight, glare, solar geometry, outdoor comfort and energy optimisation.

Having experience on managing large projects in computational design, I want to make sure that the appropriate methods and a clear target is maintained in order to develop useful and comprehensive methodologies and workflows.
